Instant Pot Pro Crisp

Week 1 Meal Plan
🌱 Dairy-Free • Beginner Friendly
📖
Monday
Learn the Basics
▼
Breakfast Pressure Cook
Hard Boiled Eggs + Toast
1. Add 1 cup water and place eggs on trivet.
2. Pressure Cook 5 min, Quick Release.
3. Ice bath 5 min. Serve with toast.
Dinner Pressure Cook
White Rice + Seasoned Chicken Thighs
Rice: 1 cup rice + 1 cup water. Pressure Cook 4 min, Natural Release 10 min.
Chicken: Season with salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder.
Pressure Cook 10 min, Natural Release 5 min.
Snack Air Crisp
Air Fryer Fries
1. Place frozen fries in air fryer basket.
2. Air Crisp 400°F, 15 min. Shake halfway.
🍲
Tuesday
One Pot Comfort
▼
Breakfast Pressure Cook
Steel Cut Oatmeal with Banana
1. Add 1 cup oats + 3 cups water + pinch salt.
2. Pressure Cook 10 min, Natural Release 10 min.
3. Top with sliced banana + cinnamon.
Dinner Pressure Cook
One-Pot Pasta
1. Add 1 lb penne + 1 jar marinara + 3 cups water + salt.
2. Stir so pasta is submerged.
3. Pressure Cook 5 min, Quick Release. Stir well.
🍟
Wednesday
Air Fryer Day
▼
Breakfast Air Crisp
Air Fryer Hash Browns + Eggs
1. Place hash browns in basket with oil spray + salt.
2. Air Crisp 400°F, 12 min, flip halfway.
3. Cook eggs on stove separately.
Dinner Combo
Crispy Chicken Thighs (Combo Move!)
1. Season chicken thighs.
2. Pressure Cook 10 min, Quick Release.
3. Transfer to air fryer basket.
4. Air Crisp 400°F, 8 min until skin is golden.
5. Serve with leftover rice.
🍜
Thursday
Soup Day
▼
Breakfast Pressure Cook
Hard Boiled Eggs + Oatmeal
Use same method as Monday (eggs) and/or Tuesday (oatmeal). Use leftovers or make fresh.
Dinner Sauté Pressure Cook
Veggie Potato Soup
1. Sauté diced onion, carrots, celery in olive oil (Sauté mode, 4 min).
2. Add diced potatoes + 4 cups broth + salt + pepper.
3. Pressure Cook 8 min, Quick Release.
4. Mash some potatoes in pot for thickness.
🌶️
Friday
Chili Night
▼
Breakfast Pressure Cook
Steel Cut Oatmeal with Blueberries
1. Same as Tuesday: 1 cup oats + 3 cups water + pinch salt.
2. Pressure Cook 10 min, Natural Release 10 min.
3. Top with blueberries + brown sugar.
Dinner Sauté Pressure Cook
Beef Chili
1. Sauté 1 lb ground beef + diced onion (Sauté mode, 5 min, break up meat).
2. Add 2 cans diced tomatoes + 1 can beans (drained) + tomato paste.
3. Season with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt.
4. Pressure Cook 20 min, Natural Release 10 min.
🎉
Saturday
Try Something New
▼
Breakfast Air Crisp
Air Fryer Sausage Links + Hash Browns
1. Place sausage in basket. Air Crisp 400°F, 10 min, turn halfway.
2. Hash browns same as Wednesday.
Dinner Pressure Cook
Leftover Chili over Rice
1. Make rice (same as Monday: 1 cup rice + 1 cup water, Pressure Cook 4 min, NR 10 min).
2. Reheat chili on Sauté mode or use Keep Warm.
Dessert Air Crisp
Air Fryer Baked Apples
1. Core 3 apples.
2. Stuff with mix of oats + brown sugar + cinnamon + coconut oil.
3. Air Crisp 350°F, 15 min.
☕
Sunday
Rest & Reward
▼
Breakfast Pressure Cook
Big Oatmeal Bowl
1. Steel cut oats (same method).
2. Top with sliced banana + blueberries + cinnamon + drizzle of oat milk.
Dinner Pressure Cook
Coconut Rice
1. Add 1 cup rice + 1 can coconut milk (top off with water to 1.5 cups liquid total).
2. Pressure Cook 4 min, Natural Release 10 min.
3. Serve with any leftover chicken or protein.
Dessert Air Crisp
Air Fryer Cinnamon Apples
1. Slice remaining apples, toss with cinnamon + brown sugar + squeeze of lemon.
2. Air Crisp 350°F, 10 min.

💡 Pro Tips

Natural Release = just let it sit, pressure drops on its own.
Quick Release = turn the valve to vent steam immediately.
Always use at least 1 cup liquid for pressure cooking.
All recipes in this plan are dairy-free.
